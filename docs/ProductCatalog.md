# Titan Steelworks Product Catalog (Summary)

## Structural Beams
- **Types**: I-Beam, H-Beam, Wide Flange  
- **Grades**: ASTM A36, A992, A572 Gr 50  
- **Sizes**: W6–W36  
- **Options**: Cut-to-length, web/flange drilling, primer coating  

## Steel Plate & Sheet
- **Types**: Hot-rolled, cold-rolled, checker plate  
- **Thickness**: 1/8" – 2" (common stock)  
- **Options**: Sheared, plasma cut, bevels, primed, galvanized  

## Reinforcement Bars (Rebar)
- **Standards**: ASTM A615 (carbon), ASTM A706 (weldable)  
- **Sizes**: #3–#11  
- **Options**: Cut-to-length, bending, tags  

## Tubing & Pipe
- **Tubing**: Square and rectangular, common gauges  
- **Pipe**: Schedule 40/80  
- **Options**: Coping, miter cuts, drilled holes, primed  

## Angles & Channels
- **Angles**: Equal/unequal, multiple thicknesses  
- **Channels**: C-Channel, MC profiles  
- **Options**: Slots, holes, brackets, primer coating
